### Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment**: Partially yes. The title and closing slides do not look AI-generated — the two-panel title, full-bleed crimson closing, and Kaiti SC + PingFang SC typography choice are all deliberate and culturally specific. But from slides 7–11, the deck enters a structural rut: identical image-right layouts, identical weight distribution, identical composition. The identical card grid in 典型应用场景 trips the banned pattern. Visual personality peaks at slide 1, disappears, resurfaces at the final slide.

### Priority Issues

**[P1] Committed Rule violated on almost every content slide.**
- Brand Crimson covers ~8–15% of content slides vs. the required 30–60%.
- Fix: Deploy the Callout Panel component (already named in design system) with brand background + white text on at least one element per slide. Add brand-colored full-width section-end bands to longer slides.
- Command: impeccable colorize slides.md

**[P1] Five-plus consecutive image-right slides create structural monotony (slides 7–12).**
- Audience predicts layout before it appears; attention drops during the deck's most differentiated content.
- Fix: Break after no more than two consecutive image-right slides. Alternate with annotated screenshot layouts, two-column comparisons, or centered stat layouts.
- Command: impeccable layout slides.md

**[P2] 支持的数据库链路 is a warehouse, not a slide.**
- 17-item source list at zoom: 0.85 is illegible at projection distance from row 3 onward.
- Fix: Cut to 8–10 most relevant databases for the audience, add "+ 更多..." tail, remove zoom hack, recover space for a CDC-approach claim.
- Command: impeccable distill slides.md

**[P2] 典型应用场景 uses the banned identical-card-grid pattern.**
- Four equal-height 2×2 cells with identical structure. Hierarchy flattened.
- Fix: Give 实时容灾 structural primacy (full-width or significantly larger with a stat callout). Use 1+3 or 3+1 layout instead of 2×2.
- Command: impeccable layout slides.md

**[P3] 感谢聆听 closing has no conversion point.**
- No contact, no QR code, no next step. Ends the pitch without closing it.
- Fix: Add contact info or QR code below divider in white on crimson.
- Command: impeccable harden slides.md
